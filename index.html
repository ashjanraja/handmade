Who am i ?

My name is Ahlam, I'm 25 years old, I graduated from the Arab American University majoring in Finance and Banking Sciences in 2017, I love embroidery, and I learned Palestinian embroidery from my mom, Then I started learning and mastering other types of embroidery such as French embroidery and Brazilian embroidery, I liked to work and be financially independent, so I started to do different types of embroidery, and I work covers, pendants for homes, accessories for girls, etc
